Upload your .apk to limited people

This is small web application to upload your .apk to limited people. IP address based limitation and Google account based limitation are available. It is useful when testing your android application.

Uploaded files will be removed after 30 days since last access.

This is open-source software. Please set up your own site if you want. The codes can be downloaded by https://github.com/tkawachi/apkuploader.
